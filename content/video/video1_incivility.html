A woman in her 30s is waiting outside the entrance to the zoo. She has long blonde hair and is wearing a brown t-shirt. She is shown from the waist up and is looking straight at you. She has a stern expression on her face and frequently switches her stance between having her arms crossed and looking impatiently around her and at her phone.

[Birds chirping in background and the hustle and bustle of a crowd]

The woman leans forward towards you.

Excuse me. Hello?

She clicks her fingers at you.

Oi. Hello. Yeah. You.

She puts her hand out, gesturing to people around her.

Yeah, I can see you’re busy. We can ALL see you’re busy.

She leans further towards you.

I want to talk to you.

She rolls her eyes as she speaks, then looks you up and down.

So how long are you gonna be?

Seriously… some of us actually want to get inside today.

She looks at her phone as she raises her voice.

Well can we hurry things along a bit?

She shakes her head before turning and speaking to people around her.

Oh, I can’t believe this.
Can you believe this?

She looks at her phone again, then back you, as her volume increases.

Honestly, what’s taking so long?
